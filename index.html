The graphic above displays the top soccer transfers of every year from 2000-2017. The size and color of a line between two points on the map indicate the number of transfer that occurred between leagues in each country. The buttons and slider below can help navigate regions and years. While the map itself is draggable and zoomable. Circles in certain countries indictate the volume of transfers between teams within the country itself.
By clicking on a particular country, you can highlight the transfers coming into and out of that particular country, and can toggle which set you would like to see using the buttons in the informational box to the left. Clicking on a line will give you information about the number of transfers from one country to another.
